PacMen Exercise



The objective of this assignment was to create two buttons: "Add PacMan" and "Start Game". "Add PacMan" will produce one or multiple Pacmen which generates them at random positions on the screen. "Start Button" then initiates the back and forth motion of the PacMen at different paces.
